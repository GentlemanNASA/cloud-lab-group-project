About Cyber G6

Student cybersecurity practitioners building real-world skills through collaborative research.

Who We Are

Cyber G6 is a campus cybersecurity collective focused on applied research, secure development, and incident response training. We bring together student talent for hands-on assessments, team-based challenges, and community defense.

Our work spans network security, application testing, and defensive monitoring, with the goal of helping student projects stay resilient while we sharpen our offensive and defensive skills.
